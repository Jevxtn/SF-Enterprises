ABOUT US


SF ENTERPRISES INC. is a small stall that will offer specialized muffins made from grated carrots. It will be located at Quezon City University
and San Francisco High School. Although we intend to start out from a small in – stall, that does not in any way stop us
from maximizing our potential in expanding business.

SF ENTERPRISES INC will be established because of the idea that carrot who has the potential to be utilized as a main ingredient
for muffin making specifically carrot may earn economic importance since this vegetables are healthy. Being creative would
be our main goal to come up with carrot muffins in different designs different variants, different themes,and
different flavors, and of course, different packaging just to appeal to our target market.

In this way children or people who do not consume carrots as a vegetable may be given a chance to love it as a muffin.
As a business, we are willing to go the extra mile to invest in building a standard SF ENTERPRISES INC.

SF ENTERPRISES INC will deliver a network for a customized product to fit into the kind of ideas we have. When it comes to
hygiene, we have put plans, processes, and structures in place that will ensure that we are always at the top
of the game when it comes to that. We will ensure that SF ENTERPRISES INC. will operate
legally and abiding by the rules and regulations of the authorities.

SF ENTERPRISES INC may earn its competitive advantage through its uniqueness from other muffins, and the taste
of the different flavors of carrot muffin that we will sell. There is hardly any customer that will
taste our muffins who would not want to come back and make more purchase –
we take delight in welcoming repeated customers over and over again.

CORE VALUES

Accountability: We acknowledge and accept responsibility for actions, products, decisions, time and policies.

and spiritually while consistently providing products of exceptional quality
and an outstanding customer service experience.
